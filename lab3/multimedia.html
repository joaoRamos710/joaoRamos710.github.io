City of Neon Dreams
In the desert’s heart, the lights never fade,
Las Vegas gleams in a shimmering cascade.
A city of fortune, of glitz and of show,
Where the dice roll free and the wild winds blow.

Bright as the stars in a velvet sky,
Tempting souls as the hours slip by.
Beneath the glow of endless lights,
Whispers of winnings and high-stakes nights.

But beyond the glamour, the laughter, the cheer,
Lingers a silence, a truth sincere.
For Vegas is magic, a fleeting glance,
A city of dreams, a city of chance.

Where riches rise and fall in a breath,
And each new bet flirts close with death.
But still they come, to dance with fate,
In this desert city, at heaven’s gate.
